## Tweet 3 — How LDP Works

The Lightning Deed Protocol transfers the *right* to claim an asset, not the asset itself.

1️⃣ Deed-Lock — Sender creates a Tapscript hash-lock on their UTXO
2️⃣ Lightning Transfer — A Lightning HTLC uses the *same* payment hash
3️⃣ Preimage Reveal — Lightning settles in milliseconds, revealing the key
4️⃣ Claim — Recipient uses the preimage to spend the asset on-chain

Atomic. Trustless. Instant.
