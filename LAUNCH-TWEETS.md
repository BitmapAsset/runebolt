## Tweet 3 (The Insight)
```
Don't move the asset. Transfer the deed.

RuneBolt uses the Lightning Deed Protocol (LDP) — it binds a Bitcoin HTLC script to a Lightning payment hash.

Same preimage. Two networks. Atomic settlement in milliseconds.

Your assets stay on Bitcoin L1 the entire time.
```

## Tweet 4 (How It Works)
```
How a RuneBolt transfer works:

1. Sender deed-locks their asset with a Tapscript (requires a preimage to unlock)
2. Lightning invoice is created with the same payment hash
3. Recipient pays the invoice — gets the preimage
4. Preimage unlocks the deed — asset claimed on-chain

Trustless. Atomic. Instant.
```
